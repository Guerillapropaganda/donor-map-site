### Who They Are

Anti-Trump conservative media outlet founded in 2018 by Bill Kristol and Charlie Sykes after The Weekly Standard closed. Funded primarily by Pierre Omidyar's Democracy Fund and other Never-Trump donors. Produces podcasts, newsletters, and analysis focused on defending democratic institutions against Trumpism.
