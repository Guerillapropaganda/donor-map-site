### Who They Are

Media company founded by Bari Weiss in 2021 (originally as a Substack newsletter, expanded to full newsroom). Series A funding of $15M from investors including Marc Andreessen, David Sacks, Howard Schultz, and Bobby Kotick. Acquired by Paramount/Skydance in October 2025 for approximately $150M. Positions itself as 'heterodox' centrist journalism challenging mainstream media orthodoxies.
