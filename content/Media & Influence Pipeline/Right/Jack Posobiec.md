### Who They Are

Jack Posobiec self-identifies as a "Republican political operative", not a journalist. He currently serves as senior editor at Human Events, a position that provides institutional credibility while his actual role remains rooted in information operations and political strategy.

His background complicates any claim to journalism. Posobiec enlisted in the Navy Reserve in May 2010 as an intelligence specialist, commissioned as a reserve intelligence officer in October 2013, and conducted ten months of HUMINT (human intelligence) analysis at Guantanamo Bay between September 2012 and mid-2013. During his tenure as a civilian analyst at the Naval Maritime Intelligence Center (2014-2017), he possessed a Top Secret security clearance. This clearance was suspended in February 2017 after allegations that he had shared classified information on Twitter. He resigned from his position shortly after.

Posobiec is fluent in Mandarin Chinese, a skill that informed his HUMINT analysis work but now sits unused in his current media role. The pattern is significant: an intelligence officer trained in classified information analysis and psychological operations doctrine now translates that methodology into domestic political content creation.
