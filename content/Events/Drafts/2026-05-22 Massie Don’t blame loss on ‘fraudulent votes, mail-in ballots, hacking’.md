## Massie: Don’t blame loss on ‘fraudulent votes, mail-in ballots, hacking’

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/5891548-massie-concedes-no-recount/)
**Date:** 2026-05-22

Rep. Thomas Massie (R-Ky.) said he won’t be asking for a recount and dismissed theories of possible voter tampering after his loss in the Kentucky GOP primary to President Trump-backed GOP candidate Ed Gallrein. “I do not believe I lost due to fraudulent votes, mail-in ballots, hacking, or mistabulated results,” Massie wrote on social media…
