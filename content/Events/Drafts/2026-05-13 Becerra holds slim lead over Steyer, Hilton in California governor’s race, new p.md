## Becerra holds slim lead over Steyer, Hilton in California governor’s race, new poll finds

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/campaign/5876037-becerra-california-governor-race-poll/)
**Date:** 2026-05-13

Former Health and Human Services (HHS) Secretary Xavier Becerra has taken a slight lead in the packed race to be California’s next governor, according to new polling data released Wednesday. The Emerson College Polling and Inside California Politics survey found the Democrat polling at 19 percent, ahead of Republican Steve Hilton and Democrat Tom Steyer. …
