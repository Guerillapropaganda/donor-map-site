## Anthropic model takedown fuels warning of ‘ad hoc’ AI regulation

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/policy/technology/5925364-ai-regulation-anthropic-trump-administration/)
**Date:** 2026-06-16

The Trump administration is coming under fire for a directive prompting Anthropic to pull its latest models, and artificial intelligence policy advocates warn the move signals the White House is taking an “ad hoc” approach to AI regulation that could hurt innovation. Anthropic disabled access to its newest Fable and Mythos models Friday after receiving a federal export control order requiring it to block foreign nationals from using them.…
