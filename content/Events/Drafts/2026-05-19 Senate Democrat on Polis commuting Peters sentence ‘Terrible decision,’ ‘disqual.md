## Senate Democrat on Polis commuting Peters sentence: ‘Terrible decision,’ ‘disqualifying’

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/senate/5884818-michael-bennet-jared-polis-tina-peters-colorado-commutation/)
**Date:** 2026-05-19

Sen. Michael Bennet (D-Colo.) says Colorado Gov. Jared Polis’s decision to commute the sentence of former county clerk Tina Peters, who was convicted of enabling a voting system breach, was “terrible” and would be “disqualifying” to Polis serving in the Senate. Bennet is one of a large number of Democrats who have panned Polis’s decision…
