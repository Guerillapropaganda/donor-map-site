## Fox News’s Brit Hume: Trump endorsement puts Texas Senate seat ‘in some danger’

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/media/5889599-trump-cornyn-paxton-senate-endorsement-brit-hume/)
**Date:** 2026-05-21

Fox News commentator Brit Hume warned on Wednesday that President Trump’s decision to back a Republican challenger in Sen. John Cornyn’s (R-Texas) reelection race could weaken the GOP’s grasp on this Senate seat. Hume called the reasoning behind Trump’s endorsement of Texas Attorney General Ken Paxton in this contest “pretty flimsy on the president’s part.”…
