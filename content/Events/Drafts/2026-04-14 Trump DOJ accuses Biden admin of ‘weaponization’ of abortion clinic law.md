## Trump DOJ accuses Biden admin of ‘weaponization’ of abortion clinic law

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/policy/healthcare/5831100-biden-trump-abortion-prosecutions-report/)
**Date:** 2026-04-14

The Trump administration in a new report Tuesday accused the Biden administration’s Justice Department of using a law historically meant to protect abortion clinics to selectively prosecute anti-abortion protesters. The report from the Justice Department’s “Weaponization Working Group” accused former Attorney General Merrick Garland of being biased in his enforcement of the Freedom of Access to Clinic Entrances (FACE) Act by aggressively charging anti-abortion activists…
