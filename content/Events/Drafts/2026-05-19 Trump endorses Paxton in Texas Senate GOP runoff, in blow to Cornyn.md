## Trump endorses Paxton in Texas Senate GOP runoff, in blow to Cornyn

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/5779709-paxton-trump-endorsement-texas/)
**Date:** 2026-05-19

President Trump has endorsed state Attorney General Ken Paxton in Texas' Senate race, snubbing incumbent senior Sen. John Cornyn (R-Texas) ahead of next week's GOP primary runoff.
