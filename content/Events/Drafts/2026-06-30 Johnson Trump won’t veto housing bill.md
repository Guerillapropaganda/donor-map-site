## Johnson: Trump won’t veto housing bill

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/house/5947075-johnson-doubts-trump-housing-bill-veto/)
**Date:** 2026-06-30

Speaker Mike Johnson (R-La.) on Monday said President Trump will not veto the bipartisan housing bill after canceling plans to sign the legislation last week. Johnson met with the president at the White House for two hours on Monday, urging him to sign the measure that aims to ensure more access to affordable housing nationwide. …
