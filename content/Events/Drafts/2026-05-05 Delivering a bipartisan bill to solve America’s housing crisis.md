## Delivering a bipartisan bill to solve America’s housing crisis

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/opinion/finance/5863384-housing-legislation-congress-action/)
**Date:** 2026-05-05

The successful Artemis II mission has inspired lawmakers to use the momentum to pass the Housing for the 21st Century Act, which aims to address the national housing affordability crisis by cutting red tape, supporting innovative housing solutions, and modernizing federal housing programs.
