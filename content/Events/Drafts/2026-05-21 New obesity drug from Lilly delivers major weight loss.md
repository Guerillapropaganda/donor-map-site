## New obesity drug from Lilly delivers major weight loss

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/policy/healthcare/5889665-eli-lilly-new-obesity-drug-weight-loss/)
**Date:** 2026-05-21

An investigational obesity drug from Eli Lilly cleared important end points in a late-stage trial, the company said Thursday, by helping patients lose up to 30 percent of their body weight. Lilly said that a once-weekly injection of retatrutide met the primary endpoint in a Phase 3 clinical trial, as well as key secondary end points for obesity, delivering clinically meaningful weight loss.
