## Pentagon reaches deal with leading AI companies for classified work

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/policy/technology/5858995-pentagon-ai-companies-classified-work-deal/)
**Date:** 2026-05-01

Eight of the country’s major artificial intelligence firms have agreed to deploying their AI systems in the Department of Defense’s (DOD) classified networks, as the agency seeks to bolster the technology into military operations.
