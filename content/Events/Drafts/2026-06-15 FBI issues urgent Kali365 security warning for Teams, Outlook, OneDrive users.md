## FBI issues urgent Kali365 security warning for Teams, Outlook, OneDrive users

**Source:** [The Hill](https://thehill.com/policy/technology/5924655-microsoft-365-phishing-threat/)
**Date:** 2026-06-15

The FBI released an urgent security warning to the public about a fast-acting scam targeting Microsoft 365 users on Teams, Outlook and OneDrive. The agency warned that the hacking platform Kali365 seeks out OAuth device codes, allowing scammers to sneak past multifactor authentication codes, and without the need for a password, to access Microsoft accounts.…
