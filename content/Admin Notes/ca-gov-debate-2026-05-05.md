# CA Gov Debate Recap — 2026-05-05 CNN debate

**Setup:** CNN nationally televised, 7 candidates on stage. Described as a "slugfest" / "knives out" after prior civil debates. Becerra was the unique attack target.

**Polling at debate (per CDP poll 2026-05-04):** Becerra and Hilton tied at 18%.

## Eight clashes worth logging

| # | Frame | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Becerra single-payer flip** | Villaraigosa: *"I've heard you say you're for it, and I've heard you say you're against."* Becerra: *"I've never said I'm against it."* California Medical Group meeting context. |
| 2 | **Hilton → Becerra McCluskie** | Hilton: *"It's a bit rich for Xavier to talk about following the law when he is mired personally in a corruption scandal."* Becerra: *"If I had been involved, the U.S. attorney would have had me in that indictment."* See section below for the full McCluskie/Williamson scheme. |

## The McCluskie / Williamson scheme — context for clash #2

| Actor | Role | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Sean McCluskie** | Becerra's longtime aide / former chief of staff | **Pleaded guilty** to conspiracy to commit bank fraud + wire fraud + conspiracy to defraud the US |
| **Dana Williamson** | **Newsom's former chief of staff** | **Indicted on 23 fraud-related charges** |
| **Becerra** | HHS Secretary 2021-2025; victim of theft from his dormant CA campaign account | Not charged. Cooperating. *"Happened outside of my vision."* |

**Scheme structure:** Williamson billed Becerra's dormant CA campaign $7,500/month for fake "consulting" → paid McCluskie $10K/month. Total $225K stolen from Becerra's dormant campaign account in 2022.

**Why this is a Newsom-Becerra continuity story:** Both political organizations had their respective chiefs of staff embedded in the same scheme stealing from one of them. The "I didn't know" defense is a hard public position for someone who ran the largest federal department.

## Beat-relevant signal

- Becerra was the **unique attack target.** He's the front-runner. Hilton + Villaraigosa + Steyer + Porter all hit him.
