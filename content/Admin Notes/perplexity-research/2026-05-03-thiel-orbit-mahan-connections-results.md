# Peter Thiel Orbit: California 2026 Political Giving and Mahan Connections

## Executive summary

The Cal-Access sweep found **NO DATA for a direct Peter Thiel contribution to Matt Mahan’s 2026 governor candidate committee or the two named pro-Mahan IE committees** in the reviewed California campaign-finance export; Thiel’s direct California state-level contribution located in the sweep was $3,000,000 to California Business Roundtable Issues PAC on December 29, 2025, not to a Mahan-linked filer ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

The Mahan-relevant Thiel-orbit money located in the records runs through adjacent Silicon Valley networks rather than Thiel personally: Brian Singerman contributed $78,400 directly to Mahan and $1,050,000 across pro-Mahan / Govern for California vehicles; Joe Lonsdale contributed $78,400 directly to Mahan and $25,000 to Steve Hilton; Reid Hoffman contributed $39,200 directly to Mahan ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

Named-publication coverage supports the “Thiel-connected benefactor network” frame but not a documented direct Thiel-Mahan relationship: the SF Chronicle described several Mahan billionaire benefactors as connected to Peter Thiel, while Politico quoted Mike Solana, affiliated with Thiel’s Founders Fund, calling Mahan “the state's sole opportunity for a rational Democrat” ([SF Chronicle](https://www.sfchronicle.com/politics/article/matt-mahan-governor-california-billionaire-22080461.php); [Politico](https://www.politico.com/news/2026/01/29/can-silicon-valley-make-a-governor-matt-mahan-is-betting-yes-00756444)).

## Method note

The audit scanned the Secretary of State Cal-Access bulk receipt records for 2024-2026 contributor names and employer strings tied to Peter Thiel, Founders Fund, Palantir, PayPal-orbit donors, and Mahan-linked filers 1486858, 1487425, 1488176, and 1450483 ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

For repeated F460/F497 references, transactions were deduplicated by filer and transaction ID where available, keeping the latest filing version; where a clean match was unavailable for common names, the report states NO DATA rather than attributing a contribution to a specific executive ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

## Structural read

The record supports a narrow formulation: Mahan has **Thiel-orbit support**, not verified **Thiel personal support**. The strongest primary-record examples are Brian Singerman’s combined $1,128,400 across Mahan-linked vehicles, Joe Lonsdale’s $78,400 direct Mahan contribution, and Reid Hoffman’s $39,200 direct Mahan contribution ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

The record does not support saying Peter Thiel himself funded Mahan in the California 2026 governor race; the located Thiel personal contribution went to California Business Roundtable Issues PAC, while the named press record frames Mahan’s orbit as Silicon Valley / Thiel-connected rather than Thiel-directed ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip); [SF Chronicle](https://www.sfchronicle.com/politics/article/matt-mahan-governor-california-billionaire-22080461.php)).
