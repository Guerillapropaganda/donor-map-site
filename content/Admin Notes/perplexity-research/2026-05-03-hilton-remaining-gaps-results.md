## Bottom line

The follow-up resolves several factual gaps but leaves the UK-citizenship dispute unresolved. The strongest new source-grounded findings are that Sierra’s latest public funding round placed it at a $10 billion valuation without OpenAI or Microsoft named as investors, Lighthouse is an actual cluster of individual employee donors rather than a corporate Hilton donor, the John McEntee donor cannot be confirmed as the Trump personnel official from permitted sources, and Hilton’s Form 700 confirms Fox News commentator income even though named reporting describes him as a former Fox host and no permitted source documents current Fox appearances while campaigning ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/09/04/bret-taylors-sierra-raises-350m-at-a-10b-valuation/); [Cal-Access DB export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip); [FPPC Form 700 public search portal](https://form700search.fppc.ca.gov/); [LA Times](https://www.latimes.com/politics/story/2026-04-22/in-uk-california-governor-candidate-steve-hilton-was-inspired-by-california)).
