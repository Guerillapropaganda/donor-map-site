# Mahan for Governor 2026: Candidate Committee Top-50 Donor Audit

## Executive summary

Mahan for Governor 2026, FPPC filer ID 1486858, shows $14,108,160.47 in gross positive receipts and $13,449,820.47 in net receipts after negative receipt adjustments/refunds in the deduplicated Cal-Access receipt records ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
There are 100 donors tied at the maximum observed aggregated candidate-committee total of $78,400, so the requested top-50 table is an alphabetical slice of a tied top tier rather than a hard dollar cutoff ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
The top 10 donors account for $784,000, or 5.56% of gross positive receipts; the top 25 account for $1,960,000, or 13.89%; and the first 50 tied top-tier donors account for $3,920,000, or 27.79% ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
Positive under-$200 receipts total $53,900.55, or 0.38% of gross positive receipts, across 554 deduplicated positive receipt transactions ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

## Method note

The audit used the Secretary of State Cal-Access bulk export and retained receipts for filer 1486858 plus overlap filers 1487425, 1488176, and 1450483 ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
Transactions were deduplicated within each filer by `TRAN_ID`, retaining the latest filing/report-date version for duplicate `TRAN_ID` records; transactions without a `TRAN_ID` used donor, date, amount, and line item as a fallback key ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
Amounts reported for donor concentration use gross positive receipts because the user-provided committee figure of roughly $14.11M matches gross positive receipts; net receipts after negative adjustments/refunds are separately stated ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).

## Limitations

This report classifies industry from disclosed employer and occupation fields in Cal-Access and does not infer non-disclosed spouses, family wealth, board roles, or private affiliations where the receipt record says N/A, Not Employed, Retired, or has no employer field ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
The top-50 table should not be treated as the complete maximum-donor universe because 100 donors tie at $78,400; a full max-donor appendix would be needed to display every donor in that tied tier ([Cal-Access bulk export](https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip)).
