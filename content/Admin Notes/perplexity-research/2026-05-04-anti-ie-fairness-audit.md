# CA Governor 2026 Anti-IE Fairness Audit

Primary dataset searched: Cal-Access bulk `FILERNAME_CD`, `CVR_CAMPAIGN_DISCLOSURE_CD`, `EXPN_CD`, `S496_CD`, `S497_CD`, and `RCPT_CD` tables from the California Secretary of State raw-data export at `https://campaignfinance.cdn.sos.ca.gov/dbwebexport.zip`.

## Bottom line

Steyer is the only candidate in the eight-candidate map with located voter-facing anti-candidate outside spending in the 2026 California state records searched. Matt Mahan has a smaller opposition-research / polling entry from Opportunity PAC, but the located Mahan spending is not equivalent to the anti-Steyer digital-media operation.

The current two-edge Steyer baseline, PG&E plus IBEW Local 1245 into committee 1490270, is directionally correct but incomplete if the map is meant to show all anti-Steyer source money. The related committee 1489677 also reports major direct receipts from JOBSPAC, the California Association of Realtors IE committee, the California Building Industry Association PAC, and CCPOA PAC.

## Steyer calibration check

The current map baseline is incomplete if it only shows PG&E Corporation and IBEW Local 1245 into committee 1490270. Those two contributors are verified and total $10.05 million, but committee 1489677 also reports the following direct anti-Steyer source money:

| Contributor | Amount |
| --- | ---: |
| Californians for Resilient and Affordable Energy / 1490270 | $10,000,000 |
| JOBSPAC, sponsored by the California Chamber of Commerce | $5,000,000 |
| California Real Estate Independent Expenditure Committee, California Association of Realtors | $5,000,000 |
| California Building Industry Association PAC | $1,000,000 |
| California Correctional Peace Officers Association PAC | $25,000 |
| **Located 1489677 receipts total** | **$21,025,000** |

The related F496/F460 anti-Steyer spending sequence for 1489677 totals $20,044,510.91 through the latest located filing sequence, including digital media, survey/research, and advance digital-media payments. The strongest map update is to preserve Steyer as the only candidate with located voter-facing anti-IE media, add the missing 1489677 source-money edges, and optionally add a separate “anti-Mahan research/polling only” edge if the map schema tracks non-public opposition research.
