Campaign website URLs are taken from the SOS official candidate contact list where present, with a few major-candidate campaign URLs added only where the contact list omitted them and marked by candidate-site URL ([California Secretary of State contact list](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/statewide-elections/2026-primary/contact-list.pdf)). ## Data gaps and unverifiable items

- **Active-campaign status for minor candidates**: Aside from candidates with direct withdrawal reporting or CNN qualification, most certified candidates have no located primary record proving active campaign operations as of May 2; those rows are marked active-campaign status `[UNVERIFIED]`.
- **Recipient committee IDs vs. candidate filer IDs**: Many Form 501 rows list a candidate filer ID but leave the recipient committee ID blank. Those rows are not converted into committee IDs; they are labeled as candidate filer IDs from CAL-ACCESS.
- **No Form 501 or late filer records**: Joel E. Jacob, Tim Nelson, Lukasz Adam Filinski, and Sam Sandak did not match a 2026 Governor Form 501 row in `F501_502_CD.TSV`; their candidate filer IDs are drawn from `FILERNAME_CD.TSV` and the declaration date is therefore treated as `[UNVERIFIED]` if a Form 501 date is required.
- **Withdrawn ballot-ghost candidates**: Eric Swalwell suspended his campaign after allegations, and Betty Yee dropped her bid on Apr. 20; both remain relevant ballot-ghost risks because the certified list was already set and CalMatters reported that names would remain on the June ballot even if candidates dropped out ([CalMatters on Swalwell](https://calmatters.org/newsletter/swalwell-governor-campaign-ends/); [CalMatters on Yee](https://calmatters.org/politics/2026/04/california-governor-race-yee/); [CalMatters field-set note](https://calmatters.org/politics/2026/03/california-governor-candidates/)).
- **Campaign website gaps**: `[UNVERIFIED]` website cells mean no website appeared in the SOS contact-list extract used for this report, not that no website exists.
- **Debate exclusions and negotiations**: CNN published its criteria and qualified list, but no primary host source located here listed candidates still negotiating or formally excluded beyond non-qualification under the stated criteria.
