## Implications

The contribution date is the day BEFORE the debate, not the day AFTER. The Resnicks paid Becerra to perform, then the law forced them to file the 24-hour disclosure the following day. The Form 497 mechanism worked as designed: the public knows because California law required disclosure within 24 hours of contributions ≥ $1,000 in the pre-election window.
