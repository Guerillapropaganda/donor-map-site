## Pacheco-Troast operational distinction

Per Gemini, Pacheco is the *Principal Officer + Treasurer of record* on the FPPC filings — but **Troast & Associates LLC is the firm receiving the consulting fees** from those PACs. So Pacheco may function as the front-of-house compliance signatory while Troast operates the campaign-strategy work. This distinction matters for the beat: it suggests Pacheco is the *name* and Troast is the *firm*. Worth verifying via:
- Pacheco's LinkedIn / employer record
- Troast & Associates LLC ownership filings
- Whether Pacheco is a Troast employee, contractor, or independent operator
