## Fix shipped

Added two source enums to `PERMISSIVE_EDGE_SOURCES` in `lib/donor-map/graph.ts`:

- **fec-oppexp** — campaign-committee → vendor monetary edges (operating expenditures: consultants, ad buyers, payroll). Vendors don't have vault profiles by construction. Same pattern as cal-access-expn. **Recovered 43,773 edges.**
- **usaspending-bulk** — federal-agency → contractor edges. Agencies often don't have vault profiles. **Recovered 911 edges.**

These sources mint name-stub nodes for the unresolved side at graph-load time, identical to the existing pattern for cal-access-bulk / fec-indiv-by-committee / icij-offshore-leaks.

## Why this matters

Each unresolved edge is silently invisible. A donor → politician edge whose donor side fails to resolve is "this politician got money from nobody" in `aggregate()`. Money goes missing from totals; relationships go missing from `neighbors()`. The 4.91% remaining is the structural gap between "we have data" and "queries see data."

For the visible site, each recovered edge is one more relationship that surfaces in `/profile`, `/money-trail`, `/thesis`, and the public graph. The 44,716 edges recovered today are a one-time gain in coverage; ongoing pruning of the residual 16,822 is editorial-cadence work.
