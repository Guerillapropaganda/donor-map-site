## Follow the Money, to Its Source

The Donor Map runs on government records, not guesswork. Our pipeline pulls from 32 data sources across federal agencies, courts, and regulatory bodies. Every number on this site traces back to an official filing.

We follow one rule: **Tier 1 First.** Government records are the foundation. Journalism provides context. Wikipedia provides identification. But the data comes from the agencies that collected it.

All pipelines run automatically **4x daily**. Data is machine-verified, then human-reviewed.

## Why This Matters

Most political money databases rely on a single aggregator. We don't. The Donor Map pulls directly from the agencies that collect the filings. FEC, Senate LDA, USASpending, SEC EDGAR, and 28 more.

**28 of our 32 sources are Tier 1 government records.** Three are academic or nonprofit databases that fill gaps the government doesn't cover. One is reference-grade for identification only.

Every pipeline runs automatically four times a day. Every data point is machine-verified against its source. Every profile is human-reviewed before it's marked ready.

Government records first. Always.
