### 4. Reconciliation 1.0 Medicaid Fallout. Implementation Timeline Approaching

The "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" (H.R.1), signed July 4, 2025, is entering its implementation phase with deadlines approaching:

**October 1, 2026:** Medicaid eligibility narrowed for certain non-US citizens
**December 31, 2026:** States required to conduct eligibility redeterminations every six months (doubled frequency)
**January 2027:** First-ever national Medicaid work requirements (80 hours/month) take effect, the single largest component of cuts ($325.8B over ten years)

CBO estimates: 11.8 million people will lose Medicaid directly + 3.1 million more lose marketplace coverage. Urban safety-net hospitals face devastating cuts to disproportionate share hospital (DSH) payments.
