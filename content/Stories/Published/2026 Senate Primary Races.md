### Who They Are

2026 Senate Primary Races. The Senate map for the 2026 midterm cycle features 33 Class II Senate seats (plus any special elections). The competitive landscape is shaped by the 2024 cycle's Republican gains and the structural dynamics of midterm elections, which historically favor the party opposing the White House. Key battleground states include: Maine (Collins retirement?), North Carolina (Tillis), Iowa (Ernst), Georgia, Texas (Cruz), and several states where MAGA vs. establishment Republican primaries will determine the general election candidate.

The 2026 primaries are the first major test of: (1) Trump's continued influence over Republican primaries while in office, (2) the crypto PAC Fairshake's second-cycle spending power, (3) whether MAGA primary challenges against incumbent Republican senators will destabilize the party's Senate majority, and (4) whether progressive primary challengers can break through Democratic Party gatekeeping in blue and purple states.
