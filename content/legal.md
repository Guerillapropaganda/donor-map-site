# Legal

The Donor Map is an open-source political donor intelligence database. Here is what you can do with it.

## Corrections

We publish corrections on any material factual error. Every claim in our profiles is traceable to a cited source. If you find an error, contact us:

- Email: guerillapropaganda@proton.me
- GitHub: [file a correction request](https://github.com/Guerillapropaganda/donor-map-site/issues/new?template=correction-request.yml)

We respond within 7 days. All corrections are logged publicly at [/corrections](/corrections).
