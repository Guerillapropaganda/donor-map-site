### Who They Are Doug Collins. 12th Secretary of Veterans Affairs (confirmed February 4, 2025, 77-23 Senate vote). Former U.S. Representative, Georgia's 9th Congressional District (2013-2021). Air Force Reserve chaplain, promoted to colonel 2023. Georgia State House (2007-2013). Baptist minister. Born 1966, Gainesville, Georgia.

### Why They Matter for the Vault Collins is the Koch network's inside man at the VA. During his House tenure, he championed three key CVA-backed bills laying groundwork for outsourcing veteran care and weakening VA employee protections. Now as Secretary, he controls a $369.3 billion annual budget and is implementing the privatization agenda that Concerned Veterans for America has pushed for 15 years. The Koch network pipeline is complete: outside advocacy (CVA) → Congressional allies (Collins in the House) → Cabinet implementation (Collins as VA Secretary). He pairs with [[_Pete Hegseth Master Profile|Pete Hegseth]] at Defense to form the two-pronged Koch network insertion into military and veteran policy.
