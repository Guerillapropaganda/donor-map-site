### RFK Jr. and the HHS Demolition. Make America Healthy Again Meets Pharma Deregulation

### Who Is RFK Jr.

Kennedy is a vaccine litigator and anti-vaccine activist who chaired Children's Health Defense (formerly World Mercury Project) from 2015 to 2023, earning over $20,000 per week. The nonprofit has filed nearly 30 federal and state lawsuits since 2020 challenging vaccines and public health mandates. He suspended his 2024 independent presidential campaign in August and endorsed Trump in Phoenix, reportedly negotiating for a Cabinet position (HHS being one of several mentioned roles) in exchange for endorsement and support that would siphon votes from Harris.

> [!money]
> Trump campaign legal team paid RFK Jr.'s law firm weeks after endorsement, suggesting transaction-based arrangement, not ideological alignment.

### HHS Confirmation and Machinery

Senate confirmed Kennedy as HHS Secretary 52-48 on February 13, 2025. He was sworn in March 19, 2025. The department controls CDC, FDA, NIH, and CMS. Kennedy immediately announced MAHA (Make America Healthy Again) as philosophical framework. Executive order signed. The contradiction: Kennedy attacks pharmaceutical companies while Trump's donor class includes massive pharma money. Big Pharma hosted "million-dollar" dinners at Mar-a-Lago during Trump's presidency. Pharmaceutical executives openly stated goals to stop Medicare drug negotiations and deregulate markets.

### The MAHA Brand vs. Reality

MAHA's stated philosophy is nutrition, exercise, and natural therapies as health foundation instead of pharmaceuticals. Official messaging: deregulation, consumer choice, voluntary corporate action. Actual implementation: the supplement and alternative health industries gained deregulation while Big Pharma's FDA enforcement weakened. Pharmaceutical companies welcomed Kennedy. PhRMA president Steve Ubl said "disruptor-in-chief" language suggested optimism for industry deregulation. The contradiction is functional: Kennedy attacks "pharma corruption" while enabling exactly what pharma wanted all along.

> [!contradiction]
> MAHA ideology: attack Big Pharma for poisoning Americans. MAHA practice: weaken FDA enforcement that would protect Americans from unproven supplements and unregulated health claims.

### FDA Restructuring and Staff Cuts

Kennedy announced 10,000 HHS staff layoffs effective May 27, 2025, with 3,500 targeting FDA. Consolidated 28 HHS divisions into 15. Reorganized smaller agencies into new "Administration for a Healthy America." Cuts targeted policy, human resources, IT, procurement, and communications staff. Drug, medical device, and food reviewers exempt from cuts. FDA shrinking by nearly 20 percent. Peter Marks, FDA's top vaccine official, resigned March 28 citing disagreement with Kennedy, stating "truth and transparency are not desired by the Secretary."

> [!quote]
> Peter Marks resignation letter: "Truth and transparency are not desired by the Secretary.". March 28, 2025.

### CDC Budget Cuts and Vaccine Program Impacts

HHS canceled $500 million in federal mRNA vaccine development contracts (COVID, flu). Rolled back $11.4 billion in COVID-era funding to state and local health departments (March 2025). Eliminated 579 state vaccination program positions. CDC lost nearly half its budget within six months. Kennedy directed CDC to remove six vaccines from routine childhood recommendation schedule. Vaccines now recommended only for "high-risk groups" or by "shared clinical decision-making" rather than universal recommendation: RSV, Hepatitis B, meningococcal, rotavirus, influenza, Hepatitis A.

### The Measles Crisis

2025 saw 2,281 confirmed measles cases, the highest in 34 years. First measles-related deaths in 10 years. United States lost its globally recognized measles "elimination status" by early 2026. As of March 2026, 1,100+ confirmed cases reported in 2026 alone with 10 new outbreaks. South Carolina epicenter: 960+ cases. Kennedy ousted all 17 CDC vaccine advisory panel members and replaced them. June 2025: court blocked Kennedy from scaling back childhood vaccine schedule (preliminary injunction maintained through May 2026 pending lawsuit). Kennedy gave "lukewarm endorsement" of measles vaccine during Texas outbreak.

### The Class Analysis: Two Masters

"Health freedom" rhetoric serves two distinct classes simultaneously. Working class people who distrust pharmaceutical industry and medical institutions gain validation: RFK Jr. gives voice to legitimate grievance (pharmaceutical corruption, FDA revolving door). Supplement and alternative health industry gains direct deregulation. Big Pharma gains weakened enforcement while maintaining legal monopoly on actual medicine. Working class people who depend on functioning public health infrastructure lose. Vaccine coverage drops → preventable disease returns → poor communities hit hardest (no money for private doctors). Food safety oversight disappears → food-borne illness clusters in working class neighborhoods. The "deregulation" language masks class transfer: from working class public health to billionaire supplement grift and pharmaceutical monopoly.

> [!money]
> FDA restructuring targets regulatory staff while protecting drug approval processes. Result: faster drug approvals, weaker safety monitoring, supplement industry deregulation without Big Pharma accountability. All three industries benefit. Only working class patients lose access to functional safety infrastructure.

### Food Safety and Nutrition Policy

Kennedy proposed eliminating sanitary requirements in industrial food processing. Weakened meat inspection, pesticide oversight, and labeling standards. Positioned as "letting people choose" between raw milk and pasteurized, unregulated supplements and FDA-approved medicine. In practice: working class families shop factory food due to price. They lose protection when safety standards vanish. Wealthy families buy organic, sourced, tested food. Food safety deregulation is a redistribution mechanism: from safe food to cheap food, from consumer protection to profit.

### HHS Workforce and DOGE Connection

280,000 to 320,000 federal positions eliminated under DOGE (which Musk left May 2025, formally disbanded November 2025). Federal spending increased ~5 percent despite cuts. Workforce reduction functional: fewer people to enforce regulations, conduct research, or challenge corporate policy. Davis-Bacon enforcement targeted (Musk DOGE). NLRB staffing cut. The machinery: cut workforce → reduce enforcement capacity → deregulation by attrition.

### The Pharma Contradiction Deepened

Kennedy met privately with pharmaceutical executives at Mar-a-Lago while attacking "Big Pharma corruption." Public rhetoric: clean up the FDA, stop corporate capture. Private reality: pharmaceutical companies openly discussed drug price deregulation, FDA approval acceleration, and weakened safety oversight. No evidence Kennedy used leverage against pharma as part of Cabinet negotiations. Instead: pharma dinners continued. Kennedy's HHS conducted drug price policy (not as harsh as Trump's public statements suggested). Pharmaceutical industry embraced Kennedy's structural weakening of FDA enforcement power, even as he attacked their "corruption" rhetorically.

### Current Status (March 2026)

Kennedy remains HHS Secretary. February 2026 leadership shakeup: Deputy Secretary Jim O'Neill departed, General Counsel Mike Stuart departing to other post, four staffers elevated. Chris Klomp (Medicare head, drug pricing chief) named as de facto Chief of Staff. Over 1,000 HHS employees have signed letter demanding Kennedy's resignation. Medical and public health leaders called for resignation. Kennedy has not resigned. MAHA movement shows internal strain: environmental advocates and vaccine skeptics who supported Kennedy become impatient with "inadequate action," while pharmaceutical companies appear willing to collaborate.
