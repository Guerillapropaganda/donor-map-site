- Kyle Bass (Hayman Capital): called Bessent "eminently more qualified"
- Larry Kudlow (former Trump NEC director): "big boost"
- [[_Steve Bannon Master Profile|Steve Bannon]]: supported Bessent

### Team Lutnick:
- [[Elon Musk|Elon Musk]]: called Bessent "a business-as-usual choice" and endorsed Lutnick

> [!money]
