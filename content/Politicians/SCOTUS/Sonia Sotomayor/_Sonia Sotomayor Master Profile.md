# Sonia Sotomayor

Associate Justice, U.S. Supreme Court (2009-present). Nominated by Barack Obama. Confirmed 68-31. First Hispanic and Latina justice. Previously served on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit (1998-2009) and U.S. District Court for the Southern District of New York (1992-1998). Born in the Bronx, New York.
