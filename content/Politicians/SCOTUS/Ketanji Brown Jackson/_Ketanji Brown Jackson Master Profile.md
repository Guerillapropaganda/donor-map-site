# Ketanji Brown Jackson

Associate Justice, U.S. Supreme Court (2022-present). Nominated by Joe Biden. Confirmed 53-47. First Black woman on the Supreme Court. Previously served on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the D.C. Circuit (2021-2022) and U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia (2013-2021). Former public defender and U.S. Sentencing Commission member.
