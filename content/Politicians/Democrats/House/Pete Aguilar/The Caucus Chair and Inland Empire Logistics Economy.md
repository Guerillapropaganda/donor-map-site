### The Inland Empire's Warehouse Economy

Aguilar's district sits in the Inland Empire — the logistics hub that processes 40%+ of all goods entering the United States through the ports of Los Angeles and Long Beach. The region's economy is dominated by massive warehouse and distribution centers operated by Amazon, Walmart, Target, and logistics companies. San Bernardino County has 4,000+ warehouses covering 1+ billion square feet — one of the highest concentrations of warehouse space in the world.

The political economy: warehouse jobs are the Inland Empire's largest employment sector, paying $15-22/hour with minimal benefits. The region has some of the worst air quality in the United States — diesel pollution from truck traffic serving the warehouses creates asthma rates 2-3x the national average. Aguilar represents workers who depend on warehouse employment for survival while suffering the health consequences of that employment.

### Leadership Fundraising and Corporate Relationships

As Caucus Chair, Aguilar is responsible for helping the DCCC raise $200+ million per cycle from corporate donors. This fundraising responsibility requires maintaining relationships with the same logistics and retail corporations that employ his constituents under conditions that progressives would describe as exploitative. The structural tension: Aguilar's district needs warehouse jobs; his leadership role requires good relationships with the companies providing those jobs; progressive policy positions (higher wages, stricter emissions standards, warehouse moratoriums) would threaten both.

> [!money]
> Aguilar's Caucus Chair position illustrates the Democratic leadership's structural dependency on corporate fundraising: the third-ranking House Democrat must raise money from corporations whose business practices harm his own constituents. Amazon's Inland Empire warehouses generate air pollution, low wages, and worker injuries — while Amazon's PAC and employee contributions flow to the same Democratic party apparatus Aguilar helps fund. The leadership pipeline selects for politicians who can manage this contradiction without resolving it.
