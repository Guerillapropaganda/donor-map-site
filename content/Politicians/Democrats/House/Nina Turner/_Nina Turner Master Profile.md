## Who She Is

Nina Turner. Former Ohio state senator (2008–2014). National co-chair of Bernie Sanders' 2020 presidential campaign. Two-time Democratic primary candidate for Ohio's 11th Congressional District (2021 special election and 2022 rematch). Both primary campaigns targeted heavily by Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI), which backed her opponent Shontel Brown. She lost both races.

Turner is the vault's earliest case study of the DMFI primary enforcement model documented in [[DMFI - Democratic Majority for Israel|DMFI's profile]]: spending $2M+ across the 2021 and 2022 cycles to defeat a progressive with a record of criticizing Israeli policy.
