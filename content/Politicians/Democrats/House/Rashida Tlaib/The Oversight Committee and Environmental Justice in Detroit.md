### Environmental Justice in Detroit's 48217

Tlaib's most constituency-relevant work is her environmental justice advocacy for Detroit's Southwest side — particularly the 48217 zip code, which the Michigan Department of Environment has identified as the state's most polluted zip code. Marathon Petroleum's Detroit refinery — the largest refinery in the Great Lakes region — sits in Tlaib's district, surrounded by predominantly Arab-American, Latino, and Black working-class communities.

Tlaib has used her Oversight Committee position to investigate Marathon's pollution, demand EPA enforcement, and advance legislation to strengthen environmental protections for frontline communities. Her "Environmental Justice for All Act" would require federal agencies to consider cumulative pollution impacts on overburdened communities — a shift from the current approach, which evaluates pollution sources individually rather than collectively.

### The Palestine Overshadow

Tlaib's environmental justice work is substantively more important to her constituents than her Palestine advocacy — but it receives a fraction of the media coverage. The media incentive structure rewards her most controversial position (Palestine) and ignores her most locally relevant work (fighting refinery pollution that causes asthma, cancer, and respiratory disease in her district).

This media dynamic serves the donor class: attention focused on Palestine means attention diverted from corporate pollution. Marathon Petroleum's PAC contributes to members of the Energy and Commerce Committee who oversee refinery regulation — but Tlaib's Oversight investigations receive less scrutiny because her Palestine positions dominate media coverage.
