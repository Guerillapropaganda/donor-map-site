## Who They Are

Robert T. Bigelow is a Las Vegas-based real-estate billionaire who founded Budget Suites of America (one of the largest extended-stay hotel chains in the US) and Bigelow Aerospace (a space-habitat manufacturer that flew two orbital demonstrator modules, Genesis I and II). He has been a major Republican donor since the 1990s and separately funded high-profile UAP/UFO research programs (BAASS, his contracts with the Defense Intelligence Agency under the AAWSAP program).
