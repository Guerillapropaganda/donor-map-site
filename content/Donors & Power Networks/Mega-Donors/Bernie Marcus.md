## Who He Was

Bernie Marcus (1929–2024). Home Depot co-founder. Republican mega-donor. Pro-Israel activist and major funder of the Israel lobby's political infrastructure. Died November 2024.

Marcus is the canonical case of Republican money flowing into Democratic primaries through the United Democracy Project super PAC. He contributed $2M+ to UDP in the 2023–2024 cycle, money that was deployed to defeat [[_Jamaal Bowman Master Profile|Jamaal Bowman]] and [[_Cori Bush Master Profile|Cori Bush]] in Democratic primaries. Marcus also gave approximately $7M directly to Donald Trump's presidential campaigns in 2016 and 2020.
