## Who They Are

James Harris Simons (1938–2024) was a mathematician and hedge-fund founder. He ran Renaissance Technologies, whose flagship Medallion Fund is one of the most profitable investment vehicles in history. Simons donated heavily to Democratic and center-left candidates and causes. particularly through the Simons Foundation, which focuses on basic research in math and sciences.
