### Who He Is

Timothy Marvin Dunn (born 1955) is a Texas oil and gas billionaire and one of the most influential Republican megadonors in the United States. He is the chief executive and co-founder of CrownQuest Operating, an oil and gas exploration and production company founded in 1996. As of October 2025, Forbes estimated Dunn's net worth at $1.5 billion.

Dunn is based in Texas and operates primarily in the Permian Basin, where CrownQuest operates oil and gas drilling operations. He is known for his Christian Nationalist alignment and for reshaping the Texas Republican Party and Texas legislature in his own ideological image.

Dunn's influence extends beyond Texas: he is increasingly active in national Republican politics through megadonor networks and has co-founded the America First Policy Institute, a think tank aligned with Trump administration priorities.
