### Who They Are

The Jewish Democratic Council of America (JDCA) is a pro-Israel advocacy organization within the Democratic Party ecosystem that claims to represent Jewish Democratic voters and donors. Unlike AIPAC, which officially maintains nonpartisan positioning, JDCA explicitly operates as a Democratic Party-aligned organization. The organization functions as a bundler of Jewish Democratic donors, an organizer of pro-Israel Democratic primary and general election support, and a voice in Democratic Party deliberations on Israel policy.

### What They Want

JDCA's core interest is ensuring that Democratic Party policy remains strongly supportive of Israel, opposing any Democratic candidates or policies that condition U.S. aid to Israel on human rights compliance or Palestinian rights. The organization wants to maintain Democratic Party consensus that Israel is a key U.S. ally deserving unconditional support despite documented human rights violations. JDCA seeks to prevent the Palestinian rights movement from gaining significant influence within Democratic Party politics.

### Who They Fund

The JDCA functions as a fundraising bundler for pro-Israel Democratic candidates, organizing donations from Jewish Democratic donors and coordinating them toward candidates who support strong U.S.-Israel alliance positions. The organization has bundled contributions to Democrats on the House Foreign Affairs Committee, Senate Foreign Relations Committee, and presidential campaigns. JDCA works to ensure that Democratic candidates prioritize pro-Israel positions even in districts with significant Palestinian American constituencies.

### What They've Gotten

JDCA has successfully maintained Democratic Party consensus on pro-Israel positions, preventing serious challenges to unconditional U.S. military aid to Israel. The organization has bundled millions in donations to Democratic candidates who oppose conditioning aid on Israeli compliance with international humanitarian law. JDCA's work has protected pro-Israel Democrats from primary challenges and has ensured that Palestinian rights advocacy remains marginal within Democratic Party structures. The organization's bundling function provides donors with efficient access to Democratic candidates and ensures those candidates understand the pro-Israel expectations of major Jewish Democratic donors.

### Class Analysis

The Jewish Democratic Council of America exemplifies how identity-based advocacy organizations can function as ethnic lobbies pursuing interests that diverge from democratic deliberation. JDCA's claim to represent "Jewish Democrats" obscures the reality that the organization represents wealthy pro-Israel Jewish donors who use their financial power to constrain Democratic Party choices on Israel policy. The organization's bundling function allows it to enforce donor preferences on Democratic candidates regardless of those candidates' districts or constituencies.
