### Who They Are

Christopher Ruddy is the founder and CEO of Newsmax, a right-wing cable news network and digital media company that has positioned itself as a Trump-aligned alternative to Fox News. Ruddy established Newsmax in 1998 but expanded the company significantly after Trump's presidency began, capitalizing on the MAGA movement and Trump's base. Ruddy has cultivated a personal relationship with Trump, becoming one of Trump's most trusted media advisors and confidants. Through Newsmax, Ruddy controls a major media platform with millions of daily viewers and subscribers.

### What They Want

Ruddy's primary interest through Newsmax is promoting Trump and Trump-aligned political figures while attacking Trump's opponents. His media company serves as a direct extension of Trump's political operation, providing favorable coverage and editorial support. Ruddy personally wants continued access to Trump and influence over Trump's decision-making, which he maintains through his position as a trusted media advisor. Newsmax wants favorable regulatory treatment as a media platform and protection from any restrictions on its operations.

### Who They Fund

Christopher Ruddy has donated to Trump's campaigns and legal defense funds, positioning himself as a Trump supporter and financial contributor. Ruddy's primary political activity occurs through Newsmax's editorial decisions rather than direct campaign donations. Newsmax employees and executives have donated to Republican candidates aligned with Trump. Ruddy uses his platform to fundraise for Trump and Trump-aligned candidates, providing them with favorable media coverage that functions as in-kind campaign contributions.

### What They've Gotten

Ruddy secured extraordinary access to Trump and influence over Trump's communications strategy through his position as a trusted advisor. Newsmax's launch and expansion coincided with Trump's political rise, demonstrating the symbiotic relationship between Trump's political power and Ruddy's business success. Ruddy's personal relationship with Trump has given him access to Trump's staff, strategy, and decision-making that most media proprietors cannot access. Newsmax has benefited from its position as Trump's preferred media outlet, generating substantial advertising revenue from Trump campaign spending and MAGA-aligned corporations.

### Class Analysis

Christopher Ruddy exemplifies how media proprietorship can be weaponized as a direct extension of a politician's power: Newsmax functions not as an independent news operation but as Trump's personal propaganda machine. Ruddy's relationship with Trump demonstrates how media ownership concentration allows wealthy individuals to merge their business interests with personal relationships to political figures, creating access and influence that exceeds what traditional campaign donations would provide. The fact that Ruddy operates Newsmax as a Trump-aligned platform while personally maintaining influence over Trump's decision-making reveals the breakdown of separation between media and political power. Unlike traditional journalists who maintain professional distance from sources, Ruddy's model is to merge media ownership with personal political power.
