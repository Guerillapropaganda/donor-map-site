### Who They Are

Anthem PAC. The political action committee of Elevance Health (formerly Anthem, formerly WellPoint), one of America's largest health insurance companies with $170+ billion in annual revenue and 47+ million members. Anthem PAC is one of the largest healthcare industry PACs, contributing $2-4M per cycle to members of Congress on the Finance, Ways & Means, Energy & Commerce, and HELP committees, the committees that control healthcare policy, insurance regulation, and Medicare/Medicaid funding.

Anthem PAC's contribution pattern is aggressively bipartisan: the PAC funds committee chairs and ranking members regardless of party, ensuring access to healthcare policymakers under any congressional configuration. The PAC's top recipients consistently include the chairs and ranking members of the committees that oversee CMS, ACA implementation, and insurance regulation, the decision-makers who determine Elevance's regulatory environment and government contract revenue.

> [!money]
> Anthem PAC is the insurance industry's committee-access purchasing system. The PAC's bipartisan contributions to healthcare committee members ensure that Elevance Health has a seat at every healthcare policy negotiation — from ACA marketplace rules to Medicare Advantage payment rates to Medicaid managed care contracts. The $2-4M per cycle investment protects $170B+ in annual revenue by ensuring that no healthcare legislation passes without industry input. The PAC's targeting precision — committee chairs and ranking members first, then subcommittee leaders, then swing votes — reveals a political operation designed to purchase legislative access, not ideological alignment.
