### Who They Are

The Tisch family is a New York-based business dynasty controlling Loews Corporation, a holding company with substantial assets in real estate, hotels (the Loews hotel chain), insurance (CNA Financial), and other operations. The family accumulated wealth through major Manhattan real estate development and hotel operations beginning in the mid-20th century. The Tischs have emerged as one of New York's most significant bipartisan political donors, contributing to both Democratic and Republican candidates across state and local politics.
