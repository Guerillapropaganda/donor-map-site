`related:` Hochul Donors, NYC Developer Interests, [[Real Estate Board of New York]], Manhattan Development, New York State Politics

### Who They Are

Moinian Group is a major New York City real estate development and management company founded by Joseph Moinian, a New York-based developer who has accumulated substantial wealth through major commercial and residential projects in Manhattan and Brooklyn. The company operates luxury residential developments, office towers, and hotel properties, positioning itself as one of the city's most active large-scale developers. Moinian personally and through his company has become a significant political donor to New York State politicians.

### What They Want

Moinian Group advances developer-class interests including: permissive zoning for large-scale development projects, tax incentive programs sustaining profitability (421-a, industrial abatement), regulatory environments facilitating project development without environmental or labor constraints, and policies supporting commercial real estate values and development activity.

### Who They Fund

Moinian distributed an estimated $3-5M in campaign contributions across 2020-2024 cycles, with major recipients including: Governor Hochul's gubernatorial campaign (Moinian emerged as one of Hochul's leading real estate donors), NYC Mayor Eric Adams' operations and political activities, state legislators controlling real estate and tax policy, and city council members favorable to development. The company also contributed to Democratic Party committees and supportive Republican candidates to maintain broad political access.

### What They've Gotten

Moinian has benefited directly from tax incentive continuation (particularly 421-a exemption maintenance critical to project profitability), zoning variances for major development projects, and regulatory support from city and state agencies. Governor Hochul's consistent support for developer-friendly policies directly correlates with Moinian's substantial campaign contributions. The company's major projects have proceeded through city planning and regulatory processes with minimal obstacles, suggesting alignment with city administrative agencies.

> [!money] Moinian's contributions to Hochul increased substantially during her 2022 gubernatorial campaign, suggesting direct transaction: Hochul receives developer funding; in return, Hochul maintains 421-a tax abatement program crucial to Moinian and industry profitability. This donation-to-policy correlation demonstrates real estate's structural power in New York.

### Class Analysis

Moinian Group exemplifies how individual developers can exercise substantial political power by converting development profits into campaign contributions. The company's $3-5M contribution level makes Moinian among New York's most significant political donors in absolute terms, yet this spending level is modest for a company controlling billions in real estate assets. The modest contribution-to-assets ratio suggests that developers operate with structural leverage over politicians independent of donation size, politicians cannot ignore real estate development interests because development activity directly affects city finances and employment. Moinian's ability to extract political favors through relatively modest contributions reveals that real estate wealth holds disproportionate political power.
