## Who They Are

Marcus is the founder of Marcus & Millichap Real Estate Investment Services (a major commercial brokerage, NYSE: MMI) and chairman emeritus of Essex Property Trust (NYSE: ESS), one of the largest multi-family REITs on the US West Coast.
