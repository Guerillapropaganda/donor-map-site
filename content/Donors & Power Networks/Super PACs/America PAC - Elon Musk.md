### Who They Are

America PAC. A Super PAC founded by Elon Musk in 2024 to support Donald Trump's presidential campaign, representing the largest single political investment by any individual in modern American history. Musk contributed $277+ million to America PAC — making it one of the most well-funded Super PACs in the 2024 cycle.

America PAC's operational model differed from traditional Super PACs: rather than running television advertising, the PAC focused on voter data collection and turnout operations — building a database of voter information through a petition website and deploying canvassers in swing states. The $1 million daily sweepstakes (giving away $1 million per day to petition signers in swing states) generated legal controversy over whether the payments constituted illegal vote-buying.

### What They Want

Trump election (achieved 2024), favorable regulatory treatment for Musk's companies (Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, xAI), DOGE (Department of Government Efficiency) appointment for Musk, reduced government regulation, and a voter data infrastructure that Musk controls for future political operations.

---

### What They've Gotten

**The DOGE Appointment:** Musk's $277 million investment in Trump's election was rewarded with leadership of the Department of Government Efficiency — an advisory body with influence over federal spending, agency staffing, and regulatory policy. Musk's DOGE position creates the most extreme conflict of interest in modern government history: the head of DOGE oversees agencies that regulate Tesla (NHTSA, EPA), award SpaceX contracts (NASA, DOD), and evaluate Neuralink's medical devices (FDA). The $277 million political investment purchased direct influence over the regulatory apparatus governing Musk's $250+ billion in business interests.
