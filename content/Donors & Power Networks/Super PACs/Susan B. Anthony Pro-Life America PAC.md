### Who They Are

Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America (formerly Susan B. Anthony List) is a major anti-abortion super PAC and advocacy organization operating both 501(c)(4) dark money and traditional Super PAC structures. The organization deployed $20M+ in 2024 electoral spending, functioning as a kingmaker in Republican Senate races and gubernatorial contests. Despite the nominal connection to women's suffrage icon Susan B. Anthony, the organization operates exclusively to advance anti-abortion policy, making it one of the most powerful single-issue PACs in American politics.

### What They Want

Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America advances total abortion prohibition without exceptions and opposition to abortion access. The organization frames abortion restriction as "protecting life" while actually serving religious conservative interests and using abortion as a mobilization vehicle for Republican electoral organizing. The PAC also opposes comprehensive sex education and contraceptive access, revealing that its mission extends beyond abortion to broader control of reproductive autonomy.

### Who They Fund

Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America deployed an estimated $20M+ in direct electoral spending across 2024, with funds flowing to: Republican Senate candidates in competitive races (particularly where Democratic incumbents support abortion access), Republican gubernatorial candidates, and ballot measure campaigns supporting abortion restrictions. The organization also operates substantial voter contact and grassroots mobilization infrastructure, mobilizing evangelical and religious conservative voters around abortion opposition.

### What They've Gotten

Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America's spending directly produced: Republican nominees in competitive Senate races explicitly opposing abortion access, state-level ballot measures passing abortion bans (2022-2024), and Supreme Court victory (overturning Roe v. Wade in 2022). The organization successfully mobilized evangelical voters to prioritize abortion in electoral choices, making abortion opposition central to Republican identity. The PAC's 2024 spending ratios made it among the most effective single-issue electoral operations in American politics in terms of policy alignment with spending.

> [!money] Susan B. Anthony Pro-Life America achieved Roe's overturn through sustained $20M+ annual spending and grassroots mobilization. The organization's successful 2024 Senate spending revealed direct abortion opposition-to-victory correlation: candidates endorsed by the PAC consistently won Senate races in competitive districts, proving anti-abortion messaging remains an effective Republican electoral tool.
