## Who They Are

The Republican Governors Association is a 527 political organization that recruits, funds, and runs ad operations for Republican gubernatorial candidates. As a 527 it must publicly disclose donors. RGA is a primary channel for corporate and wealthy-donor contributions into state-level Republican campaigns.
