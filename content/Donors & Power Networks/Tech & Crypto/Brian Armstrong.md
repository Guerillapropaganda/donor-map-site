## Who He Is

Brian Armstrong. Co-founder and CEO of Coinbase Global, Inc. — the largest U.S. cryptocurrency exchange. Personal political giving and Coinbase corporate giving combined make him one of the most significant individual figures in the crypto industry's 2024–2026 political operation.

Armstrong's role in the vault is documented primarily through [[Coinbase]] and [[Fairshake PAC]].
