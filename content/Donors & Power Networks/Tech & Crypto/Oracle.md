### Who They Are

Oracle Corporation. Enterprise software and cloud infrastructure company with $53 billion in annual revenue (2024). Co-founder [[Larry Ellison]] (net worth ~$200 billion) is one of the most politically active tech billionaires in America, hosting Trump fundraisers and contributing millions to Republican causes. Oracle's political operation combines Ellison's personal mega-donor activity with the company's aggressive pursuit of government contracts — including the TikTok deal that would make Oracle the data handler for TikTok's U.S. operations.

Oracle PAC contributes $1-2 million per cycle. Ellison's personal contributions exceed $50 million since 2016.

- [OpenSecrets: Oracle Corp organizational profile](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/oracle-corp/summary?id=D000000461) (Tier 1)

---

### What They Want

Government cloud contracts (competing with Amazon and Microsoft for Pentagon, CIA, and federal agency infrastructure), favorable positioning in the TikTok divestiture (Oracle as data trustee), reduced antitrust scrutiny of enterprise software consolidation, and AI regulatory frameworks that favor cloud infrastructure providers.

---

### What They've Gotten

**TikTok Data Partnership:** Oracle emerged as the U.S. data trustee for TikTok's "Project Texas" — storing U.S. user data on Oracle servers. The deal was facilitated by Ellison's personal relationship with Trump and Oracle's lobbying operation. The TikTok relationship generates $1.5+ billion in annual revenue for Oracle's cloud business.

**Government Cloud Contracts:** Oracle secured positions in the JWCC multi-cloud Pentagon contract alongside Amazon, Microsoft, and Google — worth billions over the contract period.

> [!money]
> Larry Ellison hosted Trump fundraisers at his Hawaiian compound, contributed $50+ million to Republican causes, and Oracle emerged as TikTok's U.S. data trustee in a deal facilitated by the Trump administration. The fundraiser-to-contract pipeline is direct: Ellison funds the president; the president directs the deal; Oracle captures the revenue.
