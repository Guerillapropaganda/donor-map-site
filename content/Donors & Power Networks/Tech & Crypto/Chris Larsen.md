## Who He Is

Chris Larsen. Co-founder of Ripple Labs and its largest individual shareholder (approximately 27% stake). One of the highest-profile cryptocurrency industry mega-donors, notable within the vault for the strategic divergence between his personal political giving and Ripple's corporate giving.

Larsen is documented in [[Fairshake PAC]] as the Ripple-adjacent donor who broke from the company's broadly bipartisan/Republican-leaning corporate strategy: while Ripple contributed approximately $49M to Fairshake PAC in 2024 (supporting largely crypto-friendly Republicans), Larsen personally donated $10M in XRP directly to the Kamala Harris campaign via Future Forward super PAC, plus $1M to Future Forward previously. This explicit Democratic alignment from Ripple's largest shareholder signals that the crypto industry's partisan strategy contains internal disagreements, and that Ripple corporate's bipartisan veneer was policy hedging, not unanimous ideology.
