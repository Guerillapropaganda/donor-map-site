### Who They Are

Conservative Partnership Institute (CPI). A 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded by former Senator Jim DeMint (R-SC) in 2017 that functions as the MAGA movement's institutional infrastructure — training conservative congressional staff, incubating allied organizations, and providing the operational backbone for Trump-aligned politics. CPI's board and leadership include Mark Meadows (Trump's former Chief of Staff), Cleta Mitchell (who participated in the January 6 pressure campaign on Georgia officials), and a network of Trump-allied operatives.

CPI's structural function is filling the gap between the traditional conservative establishment (Heritage Foundation, AEI) and the MAGA populist movement. CPI trains the staffers who run MAGA congressional offices, incubates organizations like the Election Integrity Network (Cleta Mitchell's voter fraud operation), and provides the professional infrastructure that transforms MAGA from a campaign movement into a governing apparatus. Revenue grew from $2.7M (2018) to $45M+ (2022) — reflecting the movement's institutional consolidation.

### The Shadow Infrastructure

CPI operates as a shadow personnel operation for the MAGA movement — performing the same function for Trump-aligned politics that Heritage does for mainstream conservatism. CPI's staff training pipeline ensures that MAGA-aligned offices are staffed with operatives who share the movement's priorities, creating institutional loyalty that survives individual election cycles. The organization also served as a fiscal sponsor for multiple January 6-adjacent organizations, channeling donor money to groups involved in election denial and the pressure campaign on state officials.
