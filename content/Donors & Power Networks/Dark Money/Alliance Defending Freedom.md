### Who They Are

Alliance Defending Freedom (ADF). The most successful conservative legal organization in America, with a record of 15 Supreme Court victories since 2011. ADF was founded in 1994 by evangelical leaders (including James Dobson of Focus on the Family) and has grown into a $100+ million annual operation that litigates religious liberty, anti-abortion, anti-LGBTQ, and free speech cases through a national network of 4,600+ allied attorneys.

ADF's legal strategy mirrors the NAACP Legal Defense Fund model: identify sympathetic plaintiffs, select favorable jurisdictions, build legal precedent through lower court victories, and advance cases to the Supreme Court. ADF has been the legal architect behind many of the Roberts Court's most consequential conservative decisions.

- [ProPublica: ADF nonprofit profile](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/541660459) (Tier 2)

### What They Want

Expanded religious liberty exemptions from anti-discrimination laws, abortion restrictions (post-Dobbs, ADF litigates state-level abortion cases), opposition to LGBTQ rights (marriage equality, transgender protections, adoption), school prayer and religious expression in public institutions, and parental rights in education.

---

### What They've Gotten

**Supreme Court Victories:** ADF's litigation record at the Supreme Court is the most successful of any advocacy organization in the 21st century:

- *303 Creative v. Elenis* (2023): Religious liberty exemption from public accommodation laws
- *Kennedy v. Bremerton* (2022): Public school prayer
- *Dobbs v. Jackson* (2022): ADF filed amicus supporting Roe reversal
- *Masterpiece Cakeshop v. Colorado* (2018): Religious liberty in commercial services
- *Burwell v. Hobby Lobby* (2014): Corporate religious liberty exemptions from ACA mandates

Each victory expands the legal territory in which religious objections override anti-discrimination protections — building a constitutional framework that prioritizes religious liberty over civil rights.

> ADF's $100+ million annual budget funds the most effective conservative litigation operation in America: 15 Supreme Court victories that have reshaped constitutional law on religious liberty, abortion, and LGBTQ rights. The organization's legal strategy converts conservative Christian theology into constitutional doctrine through the federal court system — using the Federalist Society's judicial pipeline to ensure sympathetic judges hear ADF's cases. The ROI: $100 million annually in organizational spending has produced constitutional precedents that will shape American law for generations.
