### What Is Not Here And Why

This section documents categories of Epstein-related claims that exist in public discourse but are deliberately excluded from this vault. The Donor Map's credibility depends on showing what we chose not to include and why. Every exclusion is a decision, and the decisions are documented.

**Flight log names without corroborating evidence.** The Epstein flight logs contain hundreds of names. Appearing on a flight log means a person flew on the plane. It does not mean they committed a crime, witnessed a crime, or had knowledge of illegal activity. The vault includes flight log data only when corroborated by additional evidence (depositions, victim testimony, financial records, or investigative reporting that establishes a substantive connection beyond transportation). Names that appear only on flight logs without additional context are held in `GP-Epstein-Research/Unverified-Holds/`.

**Social media claims and viral lists.** Every time Epstein files are released, social media fills with lists of "people named in the Epstein files." Most of these lists conflate being mentioned (as a contact, a victim, a witness, or even a person Epstein wanted to meet but never did) with being implicated in misconduct. The vault does not use social media posts as sources for factual claims. Social media posts by named public figures can be used as evidence of public statements (what someone said on record), not as evidence of underlying facts.

**QAnon-adjacent content.** The QAnon movement adopted Epstein as a central narrative element and attached his documented crimes to a broader conspiracy framework involving Satanic ritual abuse, adrenochrome harvesting, and secret military tribunals. None of this material meets any source tier standard. Claims that originate from or are primarily circulated through QAnon-adjacent channels go directly to `GP-Epstein-Research/Discredited/` regardless of whether the underlying claim might be true. If a claim has independent Tier 1 or Tier 2 verification, it enters the vault through those sources, not through QAnon channels.

**Celebrity gossip sourcing.** Claims about Epstein connections sourced exclusively to tabloids, gossip sites, unnamed "insiders," or "sources close to" formulations are held in Unverified-Holds/ unless corroborated by Tier 1 or Tier 2 reporting. Celebrity proximity to Epstein at social events (photographs at parties, galas, fundraisers) is documented as proximity, not as evidence of deeper involvement.

**Intelligence community speculation beyond documented evidence.** The Epstein case includes documented intelligence connections (Southern Air Transport as a confirmed CIA airline, Robert Maxwell as a confirmed Mossad agent, Ghislaine Maxwell's 2025 statement about Epstein having "people who helped him," the Acosta "belonged to intelligence" report). The vault documents these. The vault does not extend these documented connections into speculative frameworks about Epstein as a CIA/Mossad asset running a global blackmail operation. That theory may or may not be true. The evidence to confirm it does not currently exist at Tier 1 or Tier 2 standard. The intelligence question is framed as a question in the Epstein CIA Logistics and Protection Mechanisms notes, not as an established conclusion.

**Epstein's death.** The vault does not take a position on whether Epstein's death in custody was suicide or homicide. The official ruling is suicide. Documented irregularities exist (broken cameras, sleeping guards, removed cellmate). The vault states the official finding. It does not speculate beyond it. If future Tier 1 evidence (e.g. a reopened investigation, new forensic analysis, whistleblower testimony) changes the evidentiary picture, the vault will update.

**Underage victim identification.** The vault does not name underage victims. Where victim testimony is relevant (Maria Farmer, Virginia Roberts Giuffre, Courtney Wild), the vault uses names only for individuals who have publicly identified themselves and testified on the record. The vault does not compile lists of alleged victims from court documents for publication.
