### What This Is

The Master Donor Database. A reference node for the vault's comprehensive donor tracking system. The Donor Map Database tracks political contributions, lobbying expenditures, and donor-policy pipelines across the entire American political system, organized by sector, party, and analytical pattern.

This vault contains 1,200+ files tracking donors, politicians, PACs, corporations, dark money organizations, and analytical stories. The donor database is structured around the vault's core thesis: **donors control politicians, not the other way around.**
