### Browse by Pattern

These patterns recur across the database — across parties, chambers, decades, and industries. Once you see one, you start seeing it everywhere. Each section lists profiles where the pattern is most visible.

- [[_Jeanne Shaheen Master Profile|Jeanne Shaheen]] — Genuine Ukraine/NATO advocacy that also increases revenue for the five defense contractors in her top 20 donors
- [[_Martin Heinrich Master Profile|Martin Heinrich]] — Clean energy champion whose renewable portfolio never competes with the nuclear weapons budget he doubled
- [[_Jeff Merkley Master Profile|Jeff Merkley]] — Volcker Rule was a genuine win against Wall Street, then gutted by the regulators and subsequent administrations
- [[_Angie Craig Master Profile|Angie Craig]] — Insulin cap targeted pharmaceutical companies, not the medical device industry that funds her

- [[_Chuck Schumer Master Profile|Chuck Schumer]] — Delivers incremental progressive wins within boundaries Wall Street donors set
