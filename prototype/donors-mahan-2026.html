Mahan for Governor 2026 · the top donors.

Companion to /mahan. The full top-donor universe across the candidate committee plus the two pro-Mahan IE PACs plus the Govern for California pass-through. The reader can audit the Silicon Valley investing-class concentration directly. The concentration is not selection. It is the dataset.

The structural number is sixty-one. Sixty-one donors funded $26.95 million of the $43.2 million backing Matt Mahan in the 2026 California governor's race. The list below is who they are, in three buckets: the candidate committee top tier, the Back to Basics IE PAC, and the Deliver for California IE PAC. Plus the one-level-up donors to the Govern for California pass-through, which transferred $1.5 million into Back to Basics in April.

Every dollar on this page is verified against the California Secretary of State Cal-Access primary-source database, with cross-form transaction dedup applied. The numbers continue to update as later filings post; this page is the May 2026 snapshot.

What this list does not show

That distribution is consistent with a campaign that has a heavy max-donor base (the rows above) and a thin small-dollar long tail.
